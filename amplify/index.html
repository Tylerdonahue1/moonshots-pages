02 · What It Is

THE OSCARS
FOR OPTIMISTS.

One night. A restored 1920s movie palace in downtown Los Angeles. 1,500 builders, creators, founders, and investors in one room.

Optimism is the belief that problems are solvable, paired with the willingness to solve them. Moonshots LIVE is where that belief gets celebrated — and funded. Over $5M awarded live on stage to the people building and imagining humanity's future.

The future doesn't happen to us. We are its authors. This is the biggest party of the year for the people who believe that.

03 · What Gets Awarded

TWO PRIZES.
ONE STAGE.

$3.5M+ · Storytellers
Future Vision
XPRIZE

Filmmakers pitch three-minute visions of a future worth living in. The grand prize winner's film gets greenlit and produced with Range Media Partners. Fiction becomes the blueprint the world builds toward.

$2M · Builders
Build with
Gemini XPRIZE

Builders launch real, profitable AI businesses in 90 days — each taking on a problem that could change thousands of lives. First place takes $500K. A hackathon that ships companies.

25,000+ competitors across both. 10 finalists pitch live on stage. Everything decided that night in front of the room.
