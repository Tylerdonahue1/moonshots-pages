The Thesis

Here's what the night is really about.

Optimism isn't the belief that everything will be fine. That's naive, and it's lazy. Real optimism is the belief that problems are solvable — paired with the willingness to actually go solve them.

That combination has built every good thing we have. Every cure, every breakthrough, every leap forward started with someone who refused to accept that the problem was permanent.

September 25 is a celebration of those people. The builders and the storytellers. The ones who don't wait for the future — they make it.

The room only holds so many.
