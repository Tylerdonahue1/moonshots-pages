# Moonshot Gathering — Creator / Founder Invite Copy

Send-ready copy for personally inviting ~30 creators and entrepreneurs to the
Moonshot Gathering in LA on **September 25, 2026**. The strategy: lock in a
hand-picked founding group who'll be announced together *before* tickets open —
so the public announcement lands with momentum already behind it.

The public-commitment ask is **implied, not transactional** — people are invited
into a named founding circle that gets announced together. Saying yes *is* the
public moment.

Pair any of these with the invite page: **media.moonshots.com/invite**

### Personalized links (do this for every send)

The page reads a `?name=` parameter and stamps **"RESERVED FOR [NAME] — 1 OF 30"**
at the top, personalizes the RSVP email, and gives them a unique seat serial. It
makes each link feel bespoke and un-forwardable — which makes people far more
likely to say yes *and* screenshot/post it.

- `media.moonshots.com/invite?name=Casey%20Neistat`
- `media.moonshots.com/invite?name=Marques`

Spaces become `%20`. No name = a clean "Your seat is held" default, so the bare
link still works fine.

## Notes for personalizing each send

- **Open with something specific to them** — a recent launch, post, or project.
  One genuine line of "this is why *you*" beats any subject line.
- **Match the prize to the person.** Lead the founder/operator types with the
  Build with Gemini $1M pitch; lead the filmmakers, designers, and storytellers
  with Future Vision and "the winner's film gets made."
- **Keep the founding-group framing scarce and real** — ~30 people, announced
  together, before the public. Scarcity only works if it's true.
- **Always end on a yes/no ask** ("Can I hold your spot?") so the reply is easy.
