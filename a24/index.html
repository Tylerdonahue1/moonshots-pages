02 · The Competition

PREPARATORY
SCI-FI, AT SCALE

The Future Vision XPRIZE invites creators worldwide to produce three-minute sci-fi trailers imagining optimistic futures for humanity. Presented by Google. Administered by XPRIZE. Range Media Partners filters submissions, with a $2.5M grand prize for production funding.

It's a public R&D lab for exactly the kind of stories your financiers are funding privately — work that prepares humanity for the future, not just entertains it. 1,500 registered participants so far, including major creators like MrBeast.

"The People's Choice track surfaces talent live, in public, on an ELO leaderboard — discovery without the scouting overhead."

Range has first refusal on the grand prize winner. Range has no rights to People's Choice winners. That track is open — and it's the one the crowd is actively shaping, week over week, all the way to the live finale on September 25, 2026 in Los Angeles.

  • 01

    30-second clips, side by side

    Voters watch two clips and pick one future over the other. For A24, every vote round is a fast-read sample reel of the next director — low-commitment for audiences, high-signal for scouts.

  • 02

    ELO leaderboard updates live

    Each matchup adjusts standings using the same ranking system used in chess. Cultural traction is legible week-by-week — your team sees who's breaking out before the field does.

  • 03

    A24 owns the moment of discovery

    "A24 People's Choice" branding on the platform header, every matchup, the leaderboard, and the vote-confirmation screen. The voter sees A24 the moment they discover a new filmmaker — that association is the asset.

  • 04

    Live finale, live signal

    September 25 at the Moonshot Gathering: 1,500 attendees cast live votes from their seats as finalists screen on stage. You're watching the room react in real time — and so is everyone else.

04 · The Lane Range Can't Reach

FIRST-LOOK
ACCESS, BUILT IN

Range Media Partners has first refusal on the grand prize winner. The People's Choice winner, surfaced by the public vote, is unencumbered. That's the lane this partnership opens.

A24 sees the funnel as it forms. Real-time ELO scores. Watch standings as 100 finalists narrow to 10, then to 5. Identify breakout talent before the field does — and have a structured first-look on every name the crowd is rewarding.

"Kane Parsons was on YouTube at seventeen when A24 found him. The People's Choice track is a structured way to find the next one — in the open, with traction signal attached."

  • 01

    Co-production rights · People's Choice winner

    The crowd-selected winner is contractually open. A24 holds first-look co-production rights — a fully-funded path from public-vote breakout to A24 release.

  • 02

    Right of first conversation · all top 10

    Before agents arrive. Before the press wave. A structured intro window with every finalist the leaderboard surfaces — your scouts in the room first.

  • 03

    Real-time scouting dashboard

    Private feed of ELO standings, view counts, share velocity, and demographic signal — surfaced to your team weeks before public announcements.

  • 04

    "Discovered with A24" framing

    Public co-attribution on the People's Choice track. The narrative around any breakout names A24 — independent of whatever Range does with the grand prize.

07 · Livestream Partnership

A SHOW,
NOT A SPONSORSHIP

Per the call: a livestream collaboration sized for the registered participants and the wider audience tracking the leaderboard. Three concrete formats — pick one or stack them.

The throughline: A24's voice is on every public touchpoint where the vote is being reasoned about — not just where it's being branded.

  • 01

    Weekly Watch Party

    A recurring live show during the voting window. A24 filmmakers commentate on standout submissions, react to leaderboard shifts, and surface picks. Half taste-making, half scouting in public.

  • 02

    Pre-Finale Show

    Live broadcast in the hours before September 25. A24 hosts a roundtable with finalists, walks through the People's Choice ELO arc, and primes the room for the live vote.

  • 03

    Discovery Reel

    Short-form weekly cuts published across A24's and Moonshots' social channels — the breakout filmmakers, the matchups that surprised the leaderboard, the creators worth watching.

  • 04

    "Discovered with A24" End-Card

    A short A24 attribution card runs on every voting session and every livestream segment. Not an ad — a credit. The kind of presence that builds association without breaking flow.
