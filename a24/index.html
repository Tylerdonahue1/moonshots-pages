02 · Background

THE PEOPLE'S
CHOICE TRACK

The Future Vision XPRIZE: three-minute sci-fi films imagining optimistic futures for humanity. Presented by Google. Administered by XPRIZE.

1,500 creators registered, including MrBeast. Range Media Partners filters submissions and holds first refusal on the grand prize winner. That prize is $2.5M in production funding.

Alongside the grand prize is the People's Choice award. Public vote. ELO leaderboard. Decided live at the finale on September 25 in Los Angeles.

Range has no rights to this track. That's the lane A24 owns.
