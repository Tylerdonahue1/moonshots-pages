02 · The Competition

THE
COMPETITION.

A $3.5M global prize. Presented by Google. Administered by XPRIZE.

Creators worldwide submit three-minute sci-fi trailers imagining an optimistic future. The grand prize winner gets a greenlight — their film produced with Range Media and Republic Film, with a budget scalable toward $15M.

For 40 years, sci-fi has been overwhelmingly dystopian. FVXP is a deliberate correction. The first prize designed to bend the next decade of storytelling back toward hope.

Live finale September 25, 2026 in Los Angeles.

05 · Crowd Voting Mechanics

CROWD VOTING
MECHANICS.

Crowd voting is the public-vote track of the Future Vision XPRIZE. Anyone, anywhere can watch the finalist trailers and pick which future they want to live in. Their choices accumulate into a live leaderboard that materially weights each finalist's final score, alongside the official judging panel.
