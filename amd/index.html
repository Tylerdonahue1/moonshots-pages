Converts a one-night event into a 12-month content engine. Direct hit on Ariel's "huge reach for a fraction of brand spend" thesis. Evergreen owned content featuring the next generation of AI builders.

The Builders' Dinner
What it is

A night-before VIP reception co-hosted by AMD for 30–50 people — Peter, Astro Teller, Palmer Luckey, Lisa Su, FVXP and Hackathon finalists, Range Media, Series X founders. Intimate. No stage. Peer-to-peer.

Why it works for AMD

Positions AMD as a peer in the founder ecosystem, not a vendor. Recruiting, partnership, and brand value land in one room. Ariel is already a Series X LP, and this is the room he wants AMD inside of.

Ties AMD directly to the optimistic-future thesis and the most cinematic moment of the night. The credit lives permanently in finalist film credits, festival appearances, and Republic Film distribution. Cleanly resolves Ariel's "AI video is a little niche but interesting" hesitation.
