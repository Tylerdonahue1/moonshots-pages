02 · The Competition

THE PRIZE
THAT COUNTS
ON YOU

The Future Vision XPRIZE challenges creators worldwide to produce 3-minute sci-fi trailers imagining optimistic futures for humanity. Presented by Google. Administered by XPRIZE. The grand prize winner receives $2.5M in production funding to make their film.

The public vote is not ceremonial — it carries 20% of the final score for every finalist. The crowd shapes the outcome. And the crowd needs a home.

"The voting platform is where passive audiences become active participants — the moment a global movement becomes measurable."

SIV provides the verified, transparent voting infrastructure. Your sponsorship brands every touchpoint from launch through the live announcement on September 25, 2026 at the Moonshot Gathering in Los Angeles.

SIV uses head-to-head matchups — not star ratings, not thumbs up. Every voter watches two full trailers and picks one future over the other. This is the most engaged, most defensible format for public voting.

  • 01

    Head-to-head matchups

    Two trailers shown simultaneously. "If you could have one but not the other, which future do you want to live in?" Forces genuine engagement — you have to watch both to choose meaningfully.

  • 02

    ELO scoring updates in real time

    Each vote adjusts standings using competitive ELO ranking — the same system used in chess and sports. The leaderboard shifts with every round, driving repeat visits and social sharing.

  • 03

    Every touchpoint is sponsor-branded

    The platform header, matchup interface, leaderboard, vote-confirmation screen, and all email triggers carry your sponsorship. No competitor logos — your brand owns the moment of choice.

  • 04

    Live at the Gathering

    On September 25, 1,000+ attendees vote in real time from their seats using the same platform — projected live on stage as finalists' scores are revealed.
